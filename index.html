about

Im dedicated to developing innovative design systems. My attention to detail shapes every step of my creative process. My goal is to develop unique, high quality products.

I completed my apprenticeship as a media designer specializing in print. After this I studied design at the, specializing in communication design. I have been developing and creating print products since 2019 . Since this year I have been specializing in digital design, with the help of a bootcamp I was able to get my first insights into web development.
